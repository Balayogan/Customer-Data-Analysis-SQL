# Key Insights

## Regional Performance

- Western Europe generated the highest revenue at approximately $1.73M.
- Central America and Oceania were among the strongest performing regions.
- Central Asia generated the lowest sales revenue.

## Category Performance

- Technology was the highest revenue-generating category with approximately $4.74M in sales.
- Furniture generated over $4.11M in revenue.
- Office Supplies contributed approximately $3.79M in sales.

## Customer Segment Analysis

- Consumer customers generated approximately $6.51M in revenue.
- Corporate customers contributed approximately $3.82M.
- Home Office customers generated approximately $2.31M.

## Product Analysis

- Apple Smart Phone, Full Size was the highest-selling product.
- Smartphones dominated the top-performing product list.

## Customer Analysis

- Tom Ashbrook was the highest-value customer.

## Profitability Analysis

- Cubify CubeX 3D Printer Double Head Print generated the highest loss.
- Several products were unprofitable despite strong sales activity.
